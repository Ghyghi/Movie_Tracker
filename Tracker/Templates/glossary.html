Glossary

Here to help you understand some of the terminologies used in the app.

Some of the keywords used:

Rate
How do you rate your experience, e.g: loved, liked, hated, or even neutral
Added
When was the movie added to your list?
Genre
What kind of movie is it? You don't have to add just one.
Progress
What step are you on in your experience?
Repeat
Would you repeat it?
Start
When did you start watching it
Finish
When did you finish it
Notes
Now is the time to add your personal touch to the entry. What do you have to say about your experience?
Log
This refers to a single entry you have made to the tracker. More entries means more logs.
